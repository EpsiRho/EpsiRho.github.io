Fluff: Furry Art Viewer

Microsoft Store Github

Description

A beautiful, native windows app for e926/e621!

View, search, and download posts submitted by other users, and submit your own.

This app is in beta! There may be some issues / missing features, but I'll be updating this app fairly frequently.

Features:

  • Full control with search filters.
  • Saved Images get tags saved to their metadata.
  • Login to your e926/e621 account.
  • View and download pools.
  • View and submit comments on posts.
  • Get tag recommendation.
  • Save your favorite tags for easy access.
  • Open e621 links in app from your clipboard.
